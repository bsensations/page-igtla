ABOUT US.

We Are Brazil Sensations More than just a tour operator, we are specialists in creating experiences that awaken the senses, celebrate freedom, and leave lifelong memories. Our focus? The authentic, diverse, and vibrant Brazil — with a special affection for group travel designed for the LGBT community, where every detail is thoughtfully crafted with inclusion, joy, and safety. We believe that traveling in a group is the truest way to celebrate life: with shared laughter, unique discoveries, and deep connections. That’s why our itineraries combine comfort, exclusivity, and warm hospitality — with inclusive guides, LGBT-friendly accommodations, and immersive experiences that reflect the soul of each destination and the pace of each group. From vibrant cities to paradise beaches, from colorful parties to breathtaking natural landscapes — with a taste of local cuisine and culture along the way — we take care of every detail with passion and professionalism, so your group can simply enjoy the moment. With Brazil Sensations, you don’t just see Brazil. You experience Brazil — with authenticity, safety, and the warmth that only this country can offer.
